Business Lifecycle Process

1. Business Search: Find potential businesses using term and city search. Automatically filter out directory sites like Yelp, BBB, and Google Business listings.

2. Domain Export: Extract and normalize domain names from the search results and save them to text files for further processing.

3. Screenshot Capture: Automatically capture screenshots of business websites using Puppeteer for visual inspection.

4. Visual Review: Quickly review website screenshots to determine which businesses are good prospects for rebuilding or marketing services.

5. Email Generation: Generate highly personalized cold emails based on website content using OpenAI integration with customizable prompts.

6. Email Campaign: Execute email campaigns with proper domain warming strategies and multi-domain sending to maximize deliverability.

7. Response Analysis: Track engagement metrics and conversions to refine targeting for future campaigns, completing the business lifecycle.
